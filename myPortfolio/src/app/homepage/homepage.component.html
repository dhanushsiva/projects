This is Your Stephen King

I am an American author of horror, supernatural fiction, suspense, crime, science-fiction, and fantasy novels. Described as the "King of Horror", a play on my surname and a reference to my high standing in pop culture, my books have sold more than 350 million copies, and many have been adapted into films, television series, miniseries, and comic books. I have published 64 novels, including seven under the pen name Richard Bachman, and five non-fiction books.I have also written approximately 200 short stories, most of which have been published in book collections.
